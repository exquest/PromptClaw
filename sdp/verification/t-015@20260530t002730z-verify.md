## Correctness

`run_asset_bus_producer()` is implemented in `promptclaw/asset_bus/producer.py`. The function:
- Delegates all batch processing to `process_pending_requests_once(...)` once per poll
- Calls `clock.sleep(poll_interval_s)` between polls (not after the final poll)
- Respects `max_polls` and the `stop()` predicate
- Returns a `ProducerRunResult` with aggregate `processed`, `partial`, `failed`, and `skipped` tuples
- Validates `poll_interval_s > 0` and `max_polls > 0` (when supplied)
