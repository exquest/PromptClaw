## Correctness

All five acceptance criteria are met:

1. **arc_shape from PerformanceIntent** — `_profile_for_event` reads `arc_shape` from `event.performance_intent` (and falls back to `event.metadata`) before sampling. `test_phrase_arch_uses_performance_intent_arc_shape` confirms the explicit shape overrides sampling.

2. **Sampled from authored distribution weighted by intent_tag** — `_sample_shape` uses `random.choices` over the per-intent-tag weighted tuples in `ARC_SHAPE_DISTRIBUTIONS`. Five intent tags are defined: `statement`, `development`, `recap`, `withhold`, `release`. Unknown tags fall back to `statement`.

3. **withhold flat arc is expressive** — `flat` appears in the `withhold` distribution at weight 2.0 (equal to `inverted`). When `flat` is selected, `tempo_deviation_pct` and `amp_deviation_pct` are both zeroed (`_profile_for_event` lines). `test_phrase_arch_withhold_flat_arc_has_zero_tempo_deviation` verifies `tempo_mult == 1.0` and the metadata tag `0.0`.

4. **Shape distribution test** — `test_phrase_arch_samples_intent_weighted_shapes_over_many_phrases` runs 400 samples each for `statement` and `withhold`, asserts all five shapes appear for `withhold`, and asserts `withhold` produces more `flat` and `inverted` than `statement`.

5. **Per-phrase cache** — `_profiles_by_phrase` caches the profile keyed by `phrase_id`/`phrase_uid`/`phrase_index` or object identity, so all events in a phrase share one profile. `test_phrase_arch_samples_peak_once_per_phrase` verifies this.

`_get_base_arch` was correctly extended to accept `arc_shape` and `peak` parameters and delegates to the profile rather than self attributes.
